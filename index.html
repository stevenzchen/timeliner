Welcome to Timeliner!

Timeliner is a lightweight Java timeline application for history classes. Easily create, edit, and save graphical timelines that can be exported into image files for use in essays and printing!

$ cd your_repo_root/repo_name

Easy to Use

Timeliner is simple to use and easy to learn. Just start adding new events to your timeline, and save it so that you can work on it later! Loading your timeline from a save file is as easy as a click of the button, and creating your timeline is easy too. Enter your events in any order you want: Timeliner will sort them for you!

Generate Graphical Timelines!

Once you're ready to look at your timeline, just "create Timeline" and the program will do everything for you! Your timeline will be to scale in years, and is clean and uncluttered, with You can look at your completed timeline within the application and make edits, or, when you're happy with your product, save it as a lightweight .png image! This image can be added into a Word document or Powerpoint presentation, or even printed out.

Features

Timeline save files can be moved between computers, so you can send it to your peers for review or study! Events have descriptions hidden, and can be viewed with a hover of the mouse. If you would like, you can specify a start and end date for your timeline.

Support or Contact
